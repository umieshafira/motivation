TIPS TO STAY MOTIVATED DURING ONLINE EDUCATION

WHY ONLINE EDUCATION MOVATION IS IMPORTANT?

          UiTM was used online education method in this semester because of the county is infected with coronavirus. So the learning session and assessment will be held in online. The student have to give all the commitment to make sure this online education method went well. In this situation, the online education motivation is important to make sure that the student performance is good as learning in face to face method. It also important to student who set a goal to graduate on time and get a better pointer. Below, there are some tips that you can know to stay motivated in online study.

  1. Manage a 'To Do List' - A Wonderful Way to Succeed!

    2.      Handling a to-do list can be a great 'Backup' to get quick details about your performance level. Some students think that making a to-do list is a complete waste of time but when you study with the help of a perfectly managed to-do list, you find yourself far less stressed with a load of task to do and this will keep your learning organized.

  2. Keep Away the Negative Thoughts!

    3.      If you are thinking that you are the only person who gets overwhelmed by negative thoughts in online learning method, then you are wrong. Every single student thinks the same.It's a natural thing which only comes when you feel depressed or nervous.Negative thinking can affect your concentration and reduce your tendency to perform well in study. Try to be focused in everything you do throughout the entire day. Try to keep away your negative thought that online learning is hard. Beside, take the negative positive side about online learning. For example, by using online learning, you can study in your own and comfort environment.

  3. Set a goal!

    4.      Everyone have their on dreams and a goal that they want to achieve. Same like a student, you must have your own goal in your life. For example, your goal is you want graduate on time. So, in your mind you have set that you must to study and perform well in study to make sure that you can graduate on time. It will push you to stay motivated in online learning and give you spirit to face the challenging in online learning.


So, will you go for it or let things just stay the same? The choice is yours!

          There are some tips that can make you stay motivated during online education. I hope it will helps you to stay motivated during online education and I also hope it will encourage you to improve your study and success in your life. So, keep your motivation in online education. Who else will encourage you if not yourself 😄.
